Nanoscience and Nanotechnology Graduate Programs

Design at the atomic scale, impact in the real world.

Within the Graduate School of Natural and Applied Sciences at İzmir Kâtip Çelebi University, the Nanoscience and Nanotechnology Graduate Programs offer an interdisciplinary framework focused on nanoscale design, synthesis, characterization, and applications.
